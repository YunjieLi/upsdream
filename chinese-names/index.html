Read Chinese Names

Do you know "Zhang" is actually pronounced as "John"?
If you understand how pinyin, the standard phonetic system for Mandarin Chinese, was created, things will start to make sense.

General Rules

  • It always breaks into syllables of consonant + vowel
    • Vowel only is OK
  • There's no silent letters*
  • In names, the tones are usually dropped
