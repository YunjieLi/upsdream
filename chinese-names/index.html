A Guide to Chinese Names

Do you know "Zhang" is actually pronounced as "John"?
Here's a quick guide of how to read Mandarin names in pinyin.

General Rules

It always breaks into syllables of consonant + vowel
  • Vowel only is OK
It's an artificial system designed with minimum redundancy
  • There's no silent letters*
  • In names, the tones are usually dropped
It's the effort that counts!
  • Li is not Lie, jie is ???
  • Not even Chinese people knows all the sounds
